Committees


Student Life

The Student Life committee brings BME students together through study breaks, fun outings, and mentorship families. Families are divided into “houses” that consist of freshmen paired with upperclassmen to receive some BME-related guidance. In the spring, they organize the annual BMES barbeque for both faculty and students.

Professional Development

The Professional Development committee organizes student involvement in career-building events like Fun with Faculty and professional school panels to expose students to biotech industry and academia internships. In the spring, they will host the first on-campus biotech career fair.

Publicity

The Publicity committee manages the Columbia BMES website and the BMES bulletin board. Their responsibilities include creating event flyers and documenting these events by taking pictures. They are focusing on making the BMES website into a valuable resource for biomedical opportunities to be available to Columbia BME undergraduates.
