Columbia Biomedical Engineering Society

Who We Are


We are Columbia University’s Biomedical Engineering Society (BMES) chapter! Deciding to pursue a study in biomedical engineering can be a daunting proposition. Our organization is designed to make the process smoother. We bring together students of all levels within the field, as well as from related engineering disciplines.

Undergraduates who are interested in having a more interactive experience with the department and faculty are encourage to attend events and volunteer within the organization. Additionally, we run a mentorship program to facilitate the journey of becoming a biomedical engineer. Please check our calendar of events for information about our upcoming events, and we hope to see you soon!

Events


We have several series of events, including Fun With Faculty and Riverside Chats, as well as other social mixers, career-oriented activities, and community outreach opportunities throughout the year.

Committees

Community Outreach

The Community Outreach committee organize BMES events near or outside campus with a focus on working with other Columbia student groups like the Society of Women Engineers. In the spring, they plan to represent the department and BME student body in Relay for Life.

Student Life

The Student Life committee brings BME students together through study breaks, fun outings, and mentorship families. Families are divided into “houses” that consist of freshmen paired with upperclassmen to receive some BME-related guidance. In the spring, they will organize the annual BMES barbeque for both faculty and students.

Professional Development

The Professional Development committee organizes student involvement in career-building events like the NYC Riverside Chats and professional school panels to expose students to biotech industry and academia internships. In the spring, they will host the first on-campus biotech career fair.

Publicity

The Publicity committee manages the Columbia BMES website and the BMES bulletin board. Their responsibilities include creating event flyers and documenting these events by taking pictures. In the spring, they will focus on making the BMES website into a valuable resource and hub for all biomedical opportunities available to Columbia BME undergraduates.

Academic Affairs

The Academic Affairs committee works closely with the BME department to host events like the Fun with Faculty series and holiday department dinner, which bring faculty and students together to build strong interpersonal relationships to provide a venue for career advice. In the spring, they will host the first medical devices hackathon at Columbia University.
